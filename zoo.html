Bears


Bears are large, four-legged mammals with stocky legs, broad heads, and short tails. They have shaggy coats, small rounded ears, and plantigrade paws with five nonretractile claws.

Giraffes

The giraffe is a large African hoofed mammal belonging to the genus Giraffa. It is the tallest living terrestrial animal and the largest ruminant on Earth.
